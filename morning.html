Course Description

Students produce a daily live television broadcast for the school. They rotate through anchoring, camera operation, audio mixing, graphics, teleprompter, and directing. The course develops communication, teamwork, and media production skills in a deadline-driven environment.

Class Benefits

  • Gain real broadcast television production experience daily
  • Develop confident on-camera speaking and presentation skills
  • Learn professional video equipment and editing
  • Build teamwork in a fast-paced, deadline-driven environment
  • Create a broadcast portfolio for college applications

Requirements

  • Open to Grades 9-12
  • 5 Credits (full year)
  • No prerequisites required
