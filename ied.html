Course Description

Introduction to Engineering Design is the foundational course in the PLTW Engineering sequence. Students are immersed in the engineering design process, applying math, science, and engineering standards to hands-on projects. They work individually and in teams to design solutions using industry-standard 3D modeling software, developing technical documentation and professional presentation skills.

Class Benefits

  • Master industry-standard CAD software used by professional engineers
  • Learn the complete engineering design process from ideation to prototype
  • Develop technical documentation and professional presentation skills
  • Build teamwork and collaboration through group design challenges
  • Create a foundation for all advanced PLTW Engineering courses

Requirements

  • Open to Grades 9-10
  • 5 Credits (full year course)
  • No prerequisites required
  • PLTW Certified course
