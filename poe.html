Course Description

Honors Principles of Engineering is the second course in the PLTW sequence. This rigorous honors-level course explores multiple engineering disciplines through activity-, project-, and problem-based learning. Students investigate mechanisms, energy, statics, materials science, and control systems.

Class Benefits

  • Explore mechanical, electrical, and civil engineering disciplines
  • Apply physics and calculus to real engineering challenges
  • Build and program functional robots and automated systems
  • Develop advanced problem-solving and analytical thinking
  • Prepare for the PLTW Capstone and college engineering

Requirements

  • Open to Grades 10-12
  • 5 Credits (full year)
  • Prerequisite: IED + Algebra I (80+ recommended)
  • PLTW Certified · Honors weighted
